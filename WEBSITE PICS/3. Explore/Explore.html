The Turumba Festival

The Turumba Festival stands as a remarkable expression of faith and cultural identity in Pakil, Laguna. Rooted in centuries of devotion to Our Lady of Sorrows, it reflects the town’s enduring traditions, civic spirit, and vibrant culture.

Explore this living tradition through historical insights, community activities, and visual documentation that capture this celebrated event.

Spirit of Pakileños

The Turumba shows how the people of Pakil come together through shared faith, unity, and tradition. Families, youth, and community groups contribute their time and effort to prepare, organize, and participate in the devotion. Their unity and collective effort keeps the devotion alive.

Over time, Turumba continues to stand as a celebration of faith and a lasting symbol of Pakileños’ pride and identity.
