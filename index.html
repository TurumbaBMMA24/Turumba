The Turumba of Pakil, Laguna

Turumba sa Birhen is a religious ceremony procession held in honor of Nuestra Señora de los Dolores de Turumba
(Our Lady of Sorrows), known in Tagalog as Mahal na Ina ng Pitong Hapis ng Turumba in Pakil, Laguna.
